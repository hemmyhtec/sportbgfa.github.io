About Us

About Us

BESTOWED GRACE FOOTBALL ACADEEMY is a football school where young players, future professionals, train and learn to acquire the basic but essential skills and knowledge about the game, it challenges, expectations and goals. They are prepared adequately to deal with the challenges that come before, during and after a lucrative career. We aim to change the face of the game here, especially our mentality in regards to the beautiful game. We will try to change certain perceptions of our game, both locally and internationally.Our football school is unique, with modern ideas designed to stand our talented recruits in good stead and enable them to compete at a high level. Even at a tender age, we expose them to different scenarios from the modern game.

Aims & Objectives

The major goal of the club is helping these kids make the most of their God-given talents,eradicating the wasteful and non-profitable investments they often make in their formative years.

We are not just training them on the pitch, but also educating them about life in general, such as dealing with expectations from clubs, families, friends, nation, media, stardom and finance management. This is virtually important for African youths as these changes happen very quickly leaving many kids clueless and vulnerable at a tender age.

This is hugely important to us. We take pride in helping these kids make meaning of their young, evolving lives and limiting their vulnerability to the pressures of this world and their peers.

Bestowed Grace Football Academy will strive hard and give everything to ensure it produces a constant stream of fine professional footballers and upstanding, decent young men."



About Bestowed Grace Football Academy

Bestowed Grace Football Academy is an academy founded in 2019. It's a developmental club owned by MR.Ismail Ridwan Ayomide. The club is mainly developed for building up and to also train young boys with great potentials.
